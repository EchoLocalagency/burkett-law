Practice Areas

San Diego Family Law Practice Areas

My practice is devoted entirely to California family law in San Diego County. The eight areas below cover the matters I handle on a regular basis, from divorce and custody to guardianship and family-court navigation. If your matter falls in one of these areas, follow the link to the pillar page for more detail on how the process works in San Diego Superior Court and what an attorney does at each stage.

Written by Brian Burkett, San Diego family-law attorney · California Bar No. 220343

  • Divorce

    Filing, disclosures, negotiation, and judgment on uncontested, contested, and higher-asset dissolutions under California law. I appear on divorce matters at all four San Diego County family-law courthouses.

    Read more →

  • Child Custody

    Legal custody (decision-making) and physical custody (where the child lives), parenting plans, and modifications. California courts apply a best-interest-of-the-child standard under the Family Code, and the two of you can be sole or joint on either type.

    Read more →

  • Child Support

    California guideline child-support calculations, initial orders, modifications, and enforcement. The calculation uses statewide DissoMaster software driven by each parent's income and time share with the child.

    Read more →

  • Spousal Support

    Temporary support orders during the case and long-term support after judgment. Long-term spousal support in California is decided under Family Code section 4320, which lists the factors the court weighs for each spouse.

    Read more →

  • Mediation

    Representing you at private mediation, at San Diego Family Court Services mandatory custody mediation, or serving as a neutral mediator between parties. Mediation often settles a case without a contested hearing.

    Read more →

  • Domestic Violence

    Domestic-violence restraining orders under the California DVPA, emergency protective orders, temporary and permanent restraining orders, and the custody and support issues that follow. Filed and heard in San Diego family court.

    Read more →

  • Guardianship

    Probate guardianship of the person or estate of a minor under California Probate Code, and juvenile guardianship in dependency cases. San Diego probate guardianships are heard in the Central Courthouse Probate Division.

    Read more →

  • Family Court

    General help navigating California family court — Requests for Order, ex parte applications, judgments, enforcement, and appeals. Useful when your matter does not neatly fit any single pillar area or spans several.

    Read more →
